Meet Kiran Kumar Velmurugan: MERN Full Stack Developer and Passionate Innovator!

  Hey there, I'm Kiran, and I’m passionate about the world of technology and innovation. Currently, I’m pursuing a B.E. in Electronics and Communication at Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology, Chennai. My journey into tech started with a deep curiosity about how things work, which led me to explore various programming languages like Java, C, and Python. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in full-stack development, mastering HTML and CSS to build intuitive and responsive web applications. My love for problem-solving has driven me to work on exciting projects such as developing an AI predictive maintenance model using a random forest algorithm and transforming traditional lighting systems into smart lighting using image processing and Raspberry Pi. These projects have not only sharpened my technical skills but also fueled my passion for creating impactful solutions. I’ve had the privilege of interning at Eubix Technology Private Limited, where I gained hands-on experience and learned from industry professionals. My enthusiasm for continuous learning and innovation also led me to participate in the Smart India Hackathon 2023, where I was a finalist, showcasing a project on home automation with enhanced efficiency. Adaptability and a thirst for knowledge define me. I’m always on the lookout for new challenges that can help me grow and contribute to the tech community. Whether it's contributing to open-source projects or collaborating on innovative ideas, I’m committed to making a difference. Feel free to connect with me if you’re interested in working together on a project or just want to chat about technology and innovation!
